Who We Are

Mount Sinai Holiness Deliverance Temple

We, Mount Sinai Holiness Deliverance Temple, are a witness for Jesus Christ by preaching and teaching the gospel to train, develop, and equip believers for advancing the kingdom of God through worship, service, fellowship, outreach, and discipleship.

Join Our Family

Our Pastor

Our pastor has dedicated the last 25 years to guiding and uplifting the congregation with wisdom, compassion, and unwavering faith. Married to her beloved husband Gerome, they share a joyful and bustling family life with their three wonderful children.

Inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ, her ministry embodies love in action, reaching out to those in need with practical support and spiritual guidance. Her efforts not only provide relief to the struggling but also build a robust community rooted in kindness and understanding. Through her leadership, countless lives have been touched and transformed, reflecting her deep commitment to living out Christ's message in everyday life.
